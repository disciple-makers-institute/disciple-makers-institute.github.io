We are all Coaches

One of the most important services that DMI offers is coaching. Protocols and personnel are currently being developed, and the projected time for launching the DMI coaching program will be Spring 2020.

In the meantime, coaching opportunities will be available on a limited basis for those who are currently in leadership and training positions. Coaching can be experienced one-on-one or in a group. It can be in person, by phone, or by video conference.
